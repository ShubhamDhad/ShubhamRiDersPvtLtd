The story

What started in 1932 continues to deliver pure motorcycling experiences across generations of riders. Bullet, the quintessential Royal Enfield, is today the longest running motorcycle in history to be in continuous production. A unique, hand-painted fuel tank, the distinct thump and a standing testimony to Royal Enfield's philosophy of simple design and resilient build make Bullet a living legacy on two wheels.

Bullet 350

The ex-showroom price of the Royal Enfield Bullet 350 ranges from INR 1,50,795* to INR 1,65,715*.

The Bullet 350 offers a mileage of 37.17 kmpl.

The kerb weight of the Bullet 350 is 191 kg (ES) and 186 kg (KS)

The Bullet 350 is available in 6 colours - Onyx Black, Black, Bullet Silver available in kick-start mode and Regal Red, Royal Blue and Jet Black in electric-start mode.

The Bullet 350 has a fuel tank capacity of 13.5 litres.

The comfortable seat height of 800mm of the Bullet let's you ride in a commanding posture.
